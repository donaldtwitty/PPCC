Nursery Minsitry

At Perpetual Praise, we understand the importance of providing a safe, nurturing, and spiritually enriching environment for your children. Our team of dedicated and trained volunteers, alongside experienced staff members, are fully prepared to care for your precious little ones. We offer specialized care for various age groups, ensuring that each child receives age-appropriate attention and activities designed to foster their growth and development.

6 weeks - 9 months Weeblers: Our Weeblers program is tailored for our youngest members, providing a warm and caring environment where infants can thrive. We focus on creating a peaceful and loving space where your baby can feel secure and loved, with gentle interactions and attentive care from our trained volunteers.

10 - 23 months Wobblers: As your child begins to explore their world more actively, our Wobblers program supports their curiosity and early developmental milestones. Our caregivers engage with children through interactive play, simple stories, and songs, helping them develop motor skills and early social interactions in a safe and joyful setting.

24 - 35 months Toddlers: The Toddlers program is designed to cater to the energetic and inquisitive nature of toddlers. We offer structured activities that encourage exploration, creativity, and learning through play. Our team provides a balanced mix of guided play, storytelling, and group activities that promote social skills, sharing, and early cognitive development.

Special Needs Ministry: We believe that every child is a precious gift, and our Special Needs Ministry is dedicated to providing personalized care and support for children with unique challenges. Our trained volunteers and staff work closely with families to create a tailored experience that meets each child's individual needs. We offer a loving, inclusive environment where children of all abilities can participate in church activities, learn about God's love, and connect with others.

Weekly Small Group Study

We believe that spiritual growth is most effective within a close-knit community. Our Weekly Small Group Study provides an opportunity for you to dive deeper into your faith, build meaningful relationships, and explore the teachings of the Bible in an intimate and supportive environment.

Each week, small groups gather to study scripture, discuss its application to daily life, and encourage one another in their walk with Christ. These groups are designed to foster genuine connections, where everyone can share their thoughts, ask questions, and grow together in a safe and welcoming space.

Whether you are new to the faith, seeking answers, or looking to strengthen your relationship with God, our small groups offer a place to belong. With groups meeting at various times and locations, there’s sure to be one that fits your schedule and life stage.

Join a Weekly Small Group Study and experience the joy of learning and growing alongside fellow believers. Together, we’ll navigate life's challenges, celebrate victories, and deepen our understanding of God’s Word. This is more than just a study—a journey of faith shared with friends.

Missions Outreach

We are committed to living out the Great Commission by extending Christ's love and message beyond the walls of our church. Our Missions Outreach program is dedicated to serving communities, sharing the hope of the Gospel, and meeting the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.

Our local missions focus on making a positive impact in our surrounding communities. Whether it's through feeding the hungry, supporting local shelters, offering educational programs, or providing care to those in need, our outreach teams actively transform lives right here at home. We partner with local organizations and ministries to address the unique needs of our community, building relationships and sharing God’s love through tangible acts of service.

Get Involved
There are many ways for you to get involved in our Missions Outreach. You can participate in local outreach projects, support missionaries through prayer and financial contributions, or volunteer your time and talents. Every effort, big or small, makes a difference in advancing God’s Kingdom.

Our Missions Outreach is more than just a program—it’s a calling to be Jesus' hands and feet, to love our neighbors as ourselves, and to make disciples of all nations. We invite you to join us in this important work as we seek to bring the love of Christ to those who need it most.

Ladies Ministry

Our Ladies Ministry is a vibrant community where women of all ages and life stages come together to grow in their faith, build lasting friendships, and support one another on their spiritual journeys. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming and nurturing environment where women can explore their God-given purpose, deepen their relationship with Christ, and encourage each other through the challenges and joys of life.

Mentorship and Discipleship
We believe in the power of mentorship and discipleship as a means of spiritual growth and support. Our Ladies Ministry encourages women to walk alongside each other, sharing wisdom, guidance, and encouragement. Whether you’re looking to be mentored or to mentor someone else, we provide opportunities for women to connect meaningfully, helping each other grow in faith and life.

Join Us
We invite you to be a part of our Ladies Ministry, where you’ll find a supportive community of women passionate about their faith and each other. Together, we’ll grow in our walk with God, support one another, and make lasting memories.

Men’s Ministry

Our Men’s Ministry empowers men to live out their faith with courage, integrity, and purpose. Solid and godly men are essential to building strong families, churches, and communities. Our ministry provides opportunities for men to grow spiritually, connect, and serve the church and the broader community.

Mentorship and Accountability
We believe in the importance of mentorship and accountability in a man’s spiritual growth. Our Men’s Ministry encourages men to build relationships where they can mentor others or be mentored, providing guidance, wisdom, and support through life’s challenges. We offer opportunities for men to connect one-on-one or in small groups, fostering environments where honesty, encouragement, and accountability thrive.

Join Us
We invite you to be a part of our Men’s Ministry, where you’ll find a brotherhood of men committed to growing in their faith and living out their calling. Whether you’re seeking spiritual growth, deeper relationships, or opportunities to serve, there’s a place for you here. Together, we’ll face life’s challenges, celebrate victories, and encourage one another to become the men God has called us to be.

College and Career

Our College and Career Ministry is designed to support young adults as they navigate college life's exciting yet challenging transitions and early career stages. We understand that this season of life is filled with opportunities for growth, decision-making, and new responsibilities, and we are here to walk alongside you every step of the way.

Spiritual Growth
We are committed to helping you grow deeper in your faith during this pivotal time. Our College and Career Ministry offers Bible studies, small groups, and discipleship opportunities tailored to address this stage's unique challenges and questions. Whether exploring your faith, seeking answers, or strengthening your relationship with God, our studies provide practical insights and spiritual guidance to help you apply biblical principles in your daily life.

Mentorship and Guidance
Navigating college and early career decisions can be overwhelming, but you don’t have to do it alone. Our ministry connects young adults with mentors who offer wisdom, guidance, and support. These mentors have walked similar paths and are here to help you make informed decisions, grow personally and professionally, and encourage you in your faith journey. Whether you need career choices, academic challenges, or life balance advice, our mentors are ready to walk alongside you.

Career Development
Understanding the importance of career planning and professional growth, we offer resources and workshops to help you succeed. From resume building and interview preparation to networking opportunities and career coaching, our ministry provides tools to help you achieve your professional goals while staying grounded in your faith.

Join Us
We invite you to join our College and Career Ministry, where you’ll find a supportive community passionate about faith, friendship, and personal growth. Whether you’re navigating the challenges of college life or starting your career, we are here to support you, encourage you, and help you grow in your relationship with Christ.

Grade School



Elementary Ministry (Kindergarten - 5th Grade)
Our Elementary Ministry provides a fun, engaging, and spiritually enriching environment for kindergarten through 5th-grade children. We believe that these formative years are crucial for laying a solid foundation of faith, and we are committed to helping your child grow in their knowledge and love of Jesus.

Biblical Teaching
Through age-appropriate Bible lessons, interactive storytelling, and creative activities, we introduce children to the foundational truths of the Christian faith. Our curriculum is designed to help them understand who God is, how much He loves them, and how they can live out their faith daily. We make learning about the Bible exciting and relatable, encouraging kids to explore Scripture and apply its teachings to their lives.

Middle School Ministry (6th - 8th Grades)
Our Middle School Ministry is designed to support students in grades 6-8 as they navigate the exciting yet sometimes challenging preteen years. We create a safe, welcoming space where middle schoolers can explore their faith, build meaningful friendships, and develop a strong identity in Christ.

Biblical Foundations
We understand that middle school is a time of questioning and exploration, so our teaching focuses on helping students understand and apply biblical truths to their lives. Through engaging lessons, group discussions, and hands-on activities, we guide them in discovering what it means to follow Jesus, make wise choices, and grow in their relationship with God.

High School Ministry (9th - 12th Grades)
Our High School Ministry is focused on equipping students in grades 9-12 to own their faith and live it boldly in their everyday lives. We recognize the importance of these years as students prepare for adulthood, and we are committed to helping them develop a deep, personal relationship with Jesus.

Biblical Discipleship
Our teaching centers on helping high school students understand the depth of God’s Word and how it applies to their lives. We tackle relevant topics and real-life issues, providing biblical insights and practical advice that empower students to make godly decisions, stand firm in their faith, and positively influence their schools and communities.
